Music Sleep Timer

Description

A beautiful new design that through simplicity improved the app usability and gave it it’s own identity. Music Sleep Timer let’s you fall asleep to your favorite music. You simply start your music, and then set the count down timer. When the timer is up,Music Sleep Timer softly fades your music out and stops it. Allowing you to get your precious sleep, and stops your battery from draining.

Features:

  • Saves Mobile Battery.
  • Saves Mobile Data
  • Timer range: 10 min - 40 min.
  • Works Background.
